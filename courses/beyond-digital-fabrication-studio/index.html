Beyond Digital Fabrication Studio

A course by Krisjanis Rijnieks

Participants of the course will get an overview of tools and methods to be able to ideate and create interactive design objects by using digital fabrication and creative coding. The course is a journey into the world of open fabrication spaces, such as fab labs, makerspaces and hackerspaces; an overview of available tools will be presented along with software and methods to be used for individual creative project work.

Digital fabrication: efficient prototyping with 3D printing, laser cutting and CNC milling.

Beyond: Raspberry Pi, openFrameworks, projection mapping, sensors, Arduino.

Every student is expected to choose at least one digital fabrication and / or creative coding approach to master.
